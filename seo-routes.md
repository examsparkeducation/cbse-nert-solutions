# QSpark AI SEO Route Structure

Use lightweight static landing pages that prefill the main generator through query parameters.

## Suggested routes

- `/class-10-science`
- `/mcq-generator`

## Recommended system

1. Create a static landing page per high-intent keyword.
2. Add unique title, description, H1, FAQ, and internal links for each route.
3. Link the landing page CTA to the main app with query params.

## Example CTA links

- `/index.html?class=Class%2010&subject=Science`
- `/index.html?subject=Science`

## Content structure for `/class-10-science`

- Title: `Class 10 Science Question Generator | QSpark AI`
- Hero focused on Class 10 Science exam prep
- Sections for chapters, revision help, and FAQ
- CTA button linking into the generator with Class 10 and Science prefilled

## Content structure for `/mcq-generator`

- Title: `Free MCQ Generator for Students | QSpark AI`
- Hero focused on instant MCQ creation
- Benefits for teachers, students, and coaching centers
- CTA button linking into the generator with the chapter input in focus

## Why this works

- Each route targets a distinct keyword cluster
- Static pages load fast and are indexable
- The main generator stays centralized while the traffic entry points scale
