💡 Extra: Important Concepts for CUET 2026 Preparation

  • The "As well as / Together with" Rule: Unlike 'and', these phrases do not make the subject plural. The verb always follows the first subject. (e.g., Question 42).
  • Latin & Foreign Phrases: CUET usually picks 2-3 questions from common phrases like Ex gratia, Ab initio, Status quo, etc. Make a list of these!
  • Fixed Prepositions: Verbs like Prefer (to), Prevent (from), Die (of/from) are constants in NTA papers.
  • Figures of Speech: Don't just learn Metaphor and Simile. Advanced papers include Antithesis, Litotes, and Oxymoron.
