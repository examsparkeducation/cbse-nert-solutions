Question 1

Which of the following is responsible for the transport of water and soluble mineral nutrients from the roots to the rest of the plant?

  • A. Phloem
  • B. Xylem
  • C. Epidermis
  • D. Endodermis

Answer: B. Xylem

Explanation: Xylem is the vascular tissue responsible for unidirectional transport of water and minerals from roots to aerial parts, whereas phloem transports food.
